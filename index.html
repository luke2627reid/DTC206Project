Why This Matters

The Data Economy Is Built on You

Most people agree to hand over personal data without realizing it. Privacy policies run dozens of pages in legal language almost nobody reads. Companies like Google, Meta, TikTok, Apple, and Microsoft collect your location, browsing history, videos watched, how long you pause on content, and in some cases your voice and face. This data is used for targeted ads, building personality profiles, and sharing with third parties.


According to Security.org, Google alone collects data across more than a dozen categories. The good news: you have more control than you think.
